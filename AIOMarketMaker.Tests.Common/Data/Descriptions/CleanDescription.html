What’s inside a Poké Ball? The answer is Pokémon TCG cards, of course! Each of these cool, collectible tins opens to reveal three Pokémon Trading Card Game booster packs along with two sheets of stickers that can be used to add a touch of Pokémon flair wherever it’s needed.

The Pokémon TCG: Poké Ball Tin includes:

  • 3 Pokémon TCG booster packs
  • 2 sticker sheets
